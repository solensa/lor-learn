Extended reality (XR) XR is an umbrella term for virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR) and mixed reality (MR). VR users are fully immersed in a digital environment. AR is where digital elements are overlaid on the real world. MR is where digital elements are anchored to the real world.
  • Immersive experience leads to better knowledge assimilation and retention
  • Possible to do anything: fly, stop time, teleport etc.
  • Users can learn from failing in a safe environment
  • Innovative
  • Headsets are required
  • Takes time to set up before users can use
  • Some people experience motion sickness
